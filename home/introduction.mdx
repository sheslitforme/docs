<Info>Server prefix is set to `;` by default. Use `;prefix [symbol]` to change it for your server.</Info>

## Setting up
**-- [Join the support server to get authorized](https://evict.cc/discord)** (required if your server isn't already authorized)

**-- [Invite Evict to your server](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1203514684326805524&permissions=8&integration_type=0&scope=bot)** (invite after authorization)

## About Evict

Evict is an all in one bot dedicated to providing the best experience to the best servers on Discord.
